The Art Studio 23 LLC is a small art studio at a big community, and you can enjoy the best that both have to offer for educating, exploring and inspiring. Currently, we are focusing on the following two age groups:
  1. The 4th to 12th grade young passionate students who are willing to develop their potentials for the short-term goals of entering their dream colleges as well as their long-term goals of enjoying art through their entire colorful lives.
  2. The local adult community who are passionate and determined to spread the joys through art creations; who want to make valuable statements for their colorful lives.
Our teaching philosophy is to help students to become successful designers, artists, scholars and citizens who enhance human experience, improve the quality of life and advance environmental sustainability; and to serve as a resource for our great state and beyond through research, creative endeavors, extension and outreach.
Our teaching methods are focused on innovation, curiosity, collaboration, open exchange of ideas, diverse perspectives, and environmental and social responsibility.
